About Scarlet Waltz:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Scarlet Waltz is a Touhou-inspired 2D bullet hell game where players must dodge dense waves of projectiles while defeating a powerful boss. 
The game emphasizes precision, timing, and pattern recognition through fast-paced, high-intensity gameplay.
Jason Holtman is the founder and creator of this project.

Gameplay:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Omnidirectional movement in a 2D space
Challenging boss fight with multiple attack patterns and multiple phases
Parrying attacks to charge meters and unleashing powerful attacks

Development:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Scarlet Waltz was developed over the course of one month using GDevelop
by Jason Holtman, Hudson Lewitzke, and Orb Weaver

Key aspects of development:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Designed and implemented projectile systems and boss attack patterns

- Iterated on gameplay through third party playtesting and feedback

- Balanced difficulty based on player feedback

- Refined mechanics to improve clarity, flow, and fairness

Iteration & Playtesting:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Organized playtesting sessions with multiple players

- Gathered feedback on difficulty and game feel

- Balanced attack patterns that were perceived as unfair

Team Contributions:
